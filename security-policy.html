Security Policy

for jonahmdelaney.dev

Thanks for taking the time to help keep this site safe. I welcome good-faith reports of security issues and will make a best effort to address them promptly.

How to report

Please email: security@jonahmdelaney.dev with a brief description, impact, and steps to reproduce (screenshots or proof-of-concepts are helpful). Do not include sensitive personal data in your report.

Scope

  • This website and its static assets served from jonahmdelaney.dev and www.jonahmdelaney.dev.
  • The contact form endpoint (Google Apps Script) used by this site.

Out of scope

  • Denial of service (DoS), brute force, or automated high-volume scanning.
  • Social engineering or attacks against third parties (e.g., hosting/CDN, ISPs, email providers).
  • Best-practice recommendations without a demonstrable security impact (still appreciated—send them along!).

Testing guidelines

  • Only test against assets in scope and avoid disrupting other users.
  • Never exfiltrate, modify, or destroy data; avoid accessing content you don’t own.
  • Use test or placeholder data; don’t submit real personal information.

Disclosure & response

  • I support coordinated disclosure. Please allow reasonable time for investigation and remediation before public disclosure.
  • I’ll acknowledge your report, keep you updated on status, and notify you once a fix is deployed.
  • With your permission, I may credit you on an acknowledgments page.

Safe harbor

If you make a good-faith effort to follow this policy—only test in scope, avoid privacy harms, and report promptly—I will not pursue or support legal action against your testing activities.
